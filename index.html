The Process

From photo to card in minutes.

No design skills needed. Create a professional-grade trading card for your athlete and start raising money — all from your phone.

👤

Build Your Child's Profile

Add your child's name, activity, team or group, and a few key achievements. Sports, dance, academics, scouts — takes less than 5 minutes.

02
📸

Upload Their Photo

Upload a clear action photo. Our platform instantly composites it into a professional card template.

03
🃏

Pick Your Card Edition

Choose from Standard, Foil Edition, or Rookie Card. Each has its own design, feel, and value.

📣

Why MVP Kids Cards

More than a fundraiser.

Car washes and bake sales are exhausting. MVP Kids Cards gives your family something to be proud of — and something supporters actually want to buy.

🏅

A Keepsake That Lasts Forever

Every card is a physical artifact of this chapter of your child's athletic journey. Limited to 25 copies, beautifully printed, professionally designed.

💸

Real Money for Real Costs

Youth sports costs the average family $883 per year. A single 25-card run can raise $330+ for fees, travel, gear, and tournaments — with zero manual effort.

📈

Potential Future Value

Cards are recorded on-chain. If your athlete goes pro one day, supporters who bought their card at age 12 hold a verified piece of history — potentially worth far more.

🔒

Parent-Controlled & COPPA Safe

You control your athlete's data and likeness at all times. Full COPPA compliance, escrow-protected funds, and a 7-day refund guarantee.

⚡

Fully Automated Fulfillment

No post office runs. Cards print and ship directly to buyers within 7–10 days. Once you create your page, your job is done.

🏆

5% Royalty to Your Athlete — Forever

Every time a card resells on the secondary market, your athlete earns 5% automatically — for life — thanks to a smart contract built into every card.
