Revenue Split

Team 20% · Parent 80% Most Popular
For every card sold, 20% funds your team, 80% goes to the parent's kid. On a $29.99 Standard card: $6.00 to your team pool, $24.00 to the family.
Custom Split
You set the exact percentage. Slide to choose how to divide each card sale between your team and the family.
🏆 Team: 30% 👨‍👩‍👧 Family: 70%
Drag to adjust · Team can take 5%–50%
Team keeps 100% Max fundraising
All card revenue goes directly to your team fundraiser. Great for group fundraising drives — parents contribute by selling cards, not keeping proceeds.
Parent keeps 100%
Your team earns nothing from card sales — parents keep all proceeds for their individual athlete. Great for leagues that just want to offer the service.
