What Is a Custom Sports Card?

A custom sports card is a professionally designed collectible featuring your child — just like the cards you'd find in a pack at the store, but made entirely around your kid's photo, name, stats, and team colors. MVP Kids Cards lets any parent, coach, or league create real trading-card-quality cards for youth athletes in any sport, at any level, from recreational leagues to elite travel teams.

Our cards are printed on thick 350gsm card stock with a premium finish, making them feel like authentic collectibles. The front features your athlete's photo, name, position, team, and season. The back includes stats, a personal bio, and a unique serial number. Each card is truly one of your child's own.

What's on a Custom Sports Card?

Every card is fully customizable. You choose what information to feature, and our templates handle the professional layout automatically.

📸

Action or Portrait Photo

Upload a high-res photo from your camera roll. Action shots, team photos, or classic portrait poses all look great. Our AI crops and enhances automatically.

📊

Season Stats

Goals, batting average, points per game, personal records — add the stats that matter for your sport. Parents and grandparents love the detail.

🎨

Team Colors & Logo

Match your team's colors for an official-looking card. Upload a team logo or use our sport-specific icon library to complete the look.

🏅

Card Edition

Choose Standard, Foil, or limited Rookie edition. Each edition has a unique finish and serial numbering that makes the card feel like a real collectible.

✍️

Personal Bio

A few sentences about your athlete — favorite position, biggest achievement, or personal motto. Makes the card uniquely theirs.

🔢

Serial Number

Every card gets a unique serial number on the back (e.g., #07/25) that makes it feel like a true limited-edition collectible worth keeping.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to make a custom sports card?

The design process takes about 5–10 minutes. Once you publish your card page, family members can start ordering immediately. Physical cards ship within 5–7 business days of each order.

What photo quality do I need?

Any modern smartphone photo works well. We recommend at least 1000px on the short side. Clear lighting and a simple background produce the best results — but action shots turn out great too.

Can I make cards for a whole team?

Yes — coaches and league admins can set up a Team account that lets every family create their own card under one group. See our Teams program for details.

Do the cards look like real trading cards?

Yes. Our cards are printed on 350gsm card stock with rounded corners and a UV-coated finish — indistinguishable from professional sports cards in feel and quality.

Is my child's information safe?

Absolutely. MVP Kids Cards is COPPA-compliant. We never share or sell children's information and parents control all data. Read our COPPA compliance page for full details.
