How to Make a Custom Trading Card

Making a custom trading card for your child is easier than you think. This step-by-step guide walks you through everything — from choosing the right photo to selecting stats, picking a template, and placing your order. You can have a finished card in under 10 minutes.

Start Making Your Card Browse All Resources

Before You Start: What You'll Need

You don't need any design skills or special software. All you need is a good photo and a few minutes.

📸

A Clear Photo

High resolution (at least 1 MP). Action shots or clean portraits both work great.

📋

Player Info

Name, sport or activity, team or school, position, key stats or achievements.

🎨

A Template Preference

Browse our 22+ templates or let us suggest one based on your activity.

💳

Payment (for Orders)

Designing is free. Pay only when you're ready to order printed cards.

Step-by-Step: Making Your Card

Follow these steps to go from blank canvas to finished card.

1

Go to the Card Builder

Visit mvpkidscards.com and click "Create Free." No account required to start designing.

2

Select Your Category

Choose Sports, Dance, Academics, Arts, Scouting, Faith, eSports, or School/PTA.

3

Pick a Template

Browse templates by category. Each has a unique design, color scheme, and stats layout.

4

Upload Your Photo

Drag and drop or browse to upload. Our cropper helps you frame the shot perfectly.

5

Fill In the Details

Enter the name, stats, season, team, and any other fields on your chosen template.

6

Preview Your Card

See an instant full-resolution preview of the front and back of your card.

7

Choose an Edition

Standard, Foil, or Holographic. All ship in 3–5 business days.

8

Set Your Price & Share

Add a supporter price to fundraise, or order a personal set for your family.
