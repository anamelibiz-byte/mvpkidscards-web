Official Company Description

MVP Kids Cards is a youth athlete trading card platform that helps families fundraise and preserve their child's athletic journey. Parents create a professional trading card for their athlete in minutes — no design experience required — and share a personal fundraising page where supporters can purchase limited-edition, printed cards directly. Cards are auto-fulfilled and shipped in 7–10 days. Every card is recorded on-chain, giving athletes a permanent provenance record and a 5% royalty on every future resale — for life. MVP Kids Cards is fully COPPA compliant and parent-controlled. The company is currently in early access and based in the United States.

Key Messages for Press
  • Youth sports costs American families an average of $883 per year — MVP Kids Cards gives them a fundraiser worth being proud of, not one they have to apologize for.
  • Every MVP Kids Card is limited to 25 copies per athlete, printed at professional quality, and shipped directly to buyers — with zero manual effort from the family.
  • Cards are recorded on-chain: if a child goes on to become a professional athlete, early supporters hold a verifiable piece of history that could appreciate significantly in value.
  • We are unapologetically COPPA-first: parents control all data, all likeness, and all card settings at all times. We will never monetize children's personal information.
  • MVP Kids Cards is reinventing the sports fundraiser — turning it from an obligation into something athletes, families, and supporters are genuinely excited about.
