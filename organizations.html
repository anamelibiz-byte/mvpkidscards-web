What Partners Get

Everything Your Organization Needs to Run a World-Class Card Program

We handle printing, shipping, and payments. You focus on your kids, your community, and your goals.

💰

Commission on Every Sale

Earn a percentage of every card sold through your organization's campaigns. The more cards your community sells, the more you earn — with no cap.

🎨

Custom Org Branding

Your logo, your colors, your name. Cards and campaign pages carry your organization's brand — reinforcing identity and community pride with every purchase.

📊

Organization Dashboard

Manage all your teams, groups, and campaigns from a single dashboard. See sales totals, campaign progress, and commission earnings in real time.

📦

Bulk Order Pricing

Volume discounts on card production for organizations running multiple teams or large campaigns. The more you run, the lower your cost per card.

Shareable Campaign Links

Every team gets a unique campaign link families can share anywhere — text, email, social media. Supporters buy directly online. No cash handling. No awkward follow-ups.

COPPA-Compliant by Design

Children never create accounts. Parents control all data. Our platform meets the highest standards for youth data protection — a critical requirement for schools and districts.

🚚

Ships to Your Location

Team order packs ship directly to the coach, teacher, or organizer for easy distribution at practice, class, or meetings. No logistics headaches.
