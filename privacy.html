Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025

Plain-language summary: We collect only what we need to operate the platform. We never sell your data. We take children's privacy extremely seriously and are fully COPPA compliant. Parents have full control over their child's information and can request deletion at any time.

1. Who We Are

MVP Kids Cards ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the MVP Kids Cards platform, accessible at mvpkidscards-web.vercel.app and any associated domains. Our registered contact email is hello@mvpkidscards.com.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our platform to create athlete trading cards, fundraise, or purchase cards.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: Name, email address, phone number (optional), password.
  • Athlete profile information: Athlete's first name, last name, sport, team name, position, and statistics you choose to share. You control all athlete details.
  • Photos: Photos of your athlete that you upload for card creation. You retain ownership of all photos you upload.
  • Payment information: Billing information for purchases. Card numbers are processed directly by Stripe and are never stored on our servers.
  • Shipping information: Name and address provided by card buyers for fulfillment.
  • Communications: Messages, support requests, or feedback you send us.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, and interactions with the platform.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, device type, and screen resolution.
  • IP address: Used for security, fraud prevention, and approximate geolocation.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: See Section 6.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Payment processors: Stripe provides us with payment confirmation and basic transaction data (not full card numbers).
  • Email service providers: ConvertKit provides waitlist and email subscription data you've entered.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

We take children's privacy very seriously. MVP Kids Cards is a platform for parents and guardians to create content about their minor children — it is not a platform for children to use directly. We do not knowingly permit children under the age of 13 to create accounts or provide personal information to us.

All athlete profiles are created and managed by a verified parent or legal guardian ("Parent Account"). Children do not have independent accounts on our platform. The parent controls all data associated with their athlete, including profile information, photos, card settings, and visibility.

We collect the following information about minors only with verifiable parental consent through the parent's account: first name, last name, sport, team affiliation, statistics, and one photograph for card creation purposes.

We do not collect more information about minors than is reasonably necessary to create and sell trading cards. We do not use minor athletes' information for advertising, behavioral profiling, or any purpose other than creating and delivering the requested card.

Parents may at any time review, correct, or request deletion of their child's information by contacting us at privacy@mvpkidscards.com. Upon verified request, we will delete all data associated with a minor athlete within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g., financial transaction records).

For more information, see our dedicated COPPA Compliance page.

4. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create, manage, and operate your athlete trading card and fundraising page.
  • Process payments and fulfill physical card orders.
  • Send transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping notifications, account updates).
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Send marketing communications if you have opted in (you may opt out at any time).
  • Improve and develop our platform features and services.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Service.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.
  • Record card provenance on the blockchain (athlete's name, card edition, serial number only — no personal contact information is recorded on-chain).

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not rent or trade your personal information. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted third-party companies that help us operate the platform (e.g., Stripe for payments, our print fulfillment partner for shipping, Supabase for data storage, Vercel for hosting). These providers are contractually prohibited from using your data for any purpose other than providing services to us.
  • Card buyers: When a supporter purchases a card, we share their shipping address with our print and fulfillment partner solely for delivery purposes. We do not share buyer information with the athlete's family beyond aggregate fundraising totals.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or where we believe disclosure is necessary to protect rights, property, or safety.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of our assets, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify affected users before their data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
  • With your consent: For any other purpose with your explicit consent.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate and improve our platform. Specifically:

  • Essential cookies: Required for authentication, session management, and basic platform functionality. These cannot be disabled without breaking the platform.
  • Analytics cookies: We use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how users interact with our platform. These do not include advertising trackers or cross-site behavioral tracking.
  • Marketing cookies: Only if you have subscribed to our email list via ConvertKit. These help us understand the effectiveness of our communications.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may limit platform functionality. We do not use advertising networks or sell data to advertisers.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specifically:

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active and for 90 days after deletion, to allow for account recovery.
  • Athlete profile and photos: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of verified parental request.
  • Transaction records: Retained for 7 years to comply with financial record-keeping requirements.
  • Support communications: Retained for 2 years to help us improve customer service quality.
  • On-chain records: Card provenance data recorded on the blockchain is permanent by design and cannot be deleted. This includes only the athlete's name, card edition, and serial number — not contact information or photographs.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions (legal obligations, on-chain records).
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out of marketing: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting us.
  • Parental rights: Parents of minor athletes may review, correct, or delete their child's information at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@mvpkidscards.com. We will respond to verified requests within 30 days.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit (TLS), encrypted storage of sensitive data, access controls, and regular security reviews. Our database infrastructure is provided by Supabase, which maintains SOC 2 compliance and enterprise-grade security.

No method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

10. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Stripe — Payment processing. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — Database and authentication. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — Web hosting. Vercel Privacy Policy
  • ConvertKit — Email marketing (waitlist only). ConvertKit Privacy Policy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you by email (if you have an account) and by posting the updated policy with a new "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the platform after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@mvpkidscards.com
  • General: hello@mvpkidscards.com
  • Contact form: mvpkidscards.com/contact
