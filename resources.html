Why Choose MVP Kids Cards for Custom Trading Cards?

MVP Kids Cards is the leading platform for creating professional-quality custom trading cards for every young achiever — not just athletes. Our free online card builder makes it easy for any family, coach, teacher, or organization to design beautiful, print-ready cards in minutes. Upload a photo, choose from 22+ professional templates spanning sports, dance, academics, scouting, arts, faith milestones, eSports, and more, and order premium printed cards delivered to your door.

All cards are printed on professional trading card stock with optional holographic foil editions, and every card comes with a blockchain-verified ownership record — the same technology used by premium collector platforms — ensuring each card is authentic, numbered, and traceable. Unlike other platforms, MVP Kids Cards is built around fundraising: teams, schools, dance studios, and organizations earn real money when supporters purchase cards, with no upfront costs.

Whether you're creating personalized baseball cards for a Little League team, academic achievement cards for an honor roll ceremony, recital keepsake cards for a dance studio, or fundraising card sets for a school PTA, MVP Kids Cards provides the tools, templates, COPPA-compliant safety features, and dedicated support you need to create trading cards that feel like the real thing — because every kid deserves one.
