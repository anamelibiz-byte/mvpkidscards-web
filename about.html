Our Story

Built for the kid on the court.

MVP Kids Cards exists because every youth athlete deserves to feel like a pro — and every family deserves a fundraiser worth being proud of.

Where it started

It started at a travel basketball tournament in Houston. A parent watched their 11-year-old pour their heart out on the court — then turned around and started dreading the bake sale they had to run to pay for the next tournament. There had to be a better way.

What if the athlete was the product? What if supporters could own a piece of this journey — something real, something collectible, something that might actually be worth something one day? MVP Kids Cards was born from that question.

We built a platform that turns your child's athletic story into a limited-edition trading card. Professional design. Real print quality. Auto-fulfilled to every buyer. And a built-in fundraising engine that does the work while you cheer from the sidelines.

What we believe

🏅

Every Kid Is Already an MVP

You don't have to be going D1 to deserve a professional card. Every athlete who shows up, puts in work, and represents their team has a story worth telling.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parents Deserve Better Fundraisers

Car washes, candy bars, and GoFundMe posts are exhausting and awkward. We built something families can share with pride — and supporters genuinely want to buy.

🔒

Kids' Safety Comes First

We are fully COPPA compliant. Parents control every piece of their child's data and likeness. We will never sell, share, or profit from children's personal information.

📈

Today's Card, Tomorrow's Legacy

We record every card on-chain. If your athlete goes pro, the people who believed in them at age 12 hold a verified, provable piece of that journey — potentially worth far more than $15.

How it works

Parents create a free account, build their athlete's profile, upload a photo, and choose a card edition — Standard, Foil, or Rookie. We generate a beautiful card design in minutes and spin up a personal fundraising page with a unique link.

Supporters buy cards directly from that link. We print and ship to every buyer in 7–10 days. Proceeds go directly to the family (minus a small platform fee), and the athlete earns a 5% royalty on every future resale of their card — for life.

No inventory. No post office runs. No awkward asks. Just a link, a story, and a card worth keeping.

Where we're headed

We're currently in early access, onboarding our first families. Our roadmap includes team and league packages, coach dashboards, advanced card customization, and eventually a secondary marketplace where collector cards can be traded — with athletes always earning their cut.

We believe the sports trading card industry is ripe for reinvention — and it should start with the youngest athletes, not end with them.
