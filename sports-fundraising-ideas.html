The Best Sports Fundraising Ideas Ranked

Not all fundraisers are created equal. Here's an honest breakdown of what works and what wastes your weekend.

🃏

Custom Trading Cards (MVP)

Avg. $3,000+ per campaign. $0 upfront. Families love them. Kids are proud. Repeat donors every season.

🍫

Candy & Food Sales

Easy to start, but margins are thin (~$1/item), and families dread the door-knocking.

🚗

Car Washes

Fun but weather-dependent, labor-intensive, and low earning ceiling per team.

🍕

Restaurant Nights

Easy — but 15–20% of one night's sales isn't going to fund tournament travel.

🎟️

Raffle Tickets

Can earn well with big prizes, but requires prize donations and more logistics.

🏃

Walk-a-Thons / Fun Runs

Great for schools. Teams need a dedicated space and lots of coordination.

Why Card Fundraisers Win

Trading card fundraisers outperform traditional fundraisers in almost every category that matters to teams and families.

1

No Upfront Cost

Unlike candy or raffle prizes, you pay nothing until orders come in.

2

Kids Are Proud to Share

Nobody's ashamed to text grandma their own custom trading card link. Shares happen naturally.

3

High Per-Card Earnings

Teams typically net $12–$18 per card. Candy bars net $1. The math is obvious.

4

Works Online

No door-knocking. Share a link by text, social media, or email. Supporters order from anywhere.

5

Runs All Year

Launch a new card for each season and fundraise year-round with returning supporters.
