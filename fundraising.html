Step by Step

From Idea to "We Raised How Much?!" in Five Steps

Setting up your campaign takes about two minutes. Everything else runs itself.

1

Create Your Campaign

Sign up free and set up your group — name, category (sport, dance, school, etc.), number of participants, and your fundraising goal.

2

Everyone Gets Their Card

Each participant (or their parent) uploads a photo and customizes their card. Sports stats, academic achievements, dance style — it all fits.

3

Share the Link

Every family gets a shareable card link. Text it to grandma, post it in the family group chat, share it on Facebook. Cards sell themselves.

4

Supporters Buy Online

Family and friends purchase cards directly — secure, simple checkout. No collecting cash at practice or chasing down payments.

5

Cards Ship, Funds Land

Cards ship to your team organizer for distribution at the next practice or meeting. Fundraising proceeds are transferred to your organization.
