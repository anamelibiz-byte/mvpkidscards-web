Quick answers to common questions

When will I get access after joining the waitlist?
We're onboarding families in waves, typically within 1–2 weeks of joining. We'll email you directly with next steps when your spot is ready.
How long does it take for cards to ship?
Cards are printed and shipped within 7–10 business days of purchase. Buyers receive a tracking number automatically via email.
Can teams or leagues use MVP Kids Cards?
Absolutely — team and league packages are coming soon. Email us at partners@mvpkidscards.com and we'll set you up with a custom arrangement in the meantime.
Is my child's photo and data safe?
Yes. We are fully COPPA compliant. Parents control all data and likeness settings. We never sell, share, or profit from children's personal information. See our COPPA Compliance page for details.
What's your refund policy?
We offer a 7-day refund window for digital purchases. Physical cards that have already been printed and shipped cannot be refunded. See our Refund Policy for full details.
