What Makes a Great Trading Card?

The best custom trading cards combine a great photo, clean design, and meaningful stats or achievements. Our templates are designed by professionals who know what makes a card feel authentic and collectible.

1

Start With a Great Photo

Action shots, recital photos, graduation portraits — any moment that captures your child at their best.

2

Choose Your Category Template

Pick from 22+ templates designed for your specific activity — sports, dance, academics, and more.

3

Add Stats or Achievements

Sports use game stats. Dance uses performance highlights. Academics use GPA, awards, and activities.

4

Select Your Edition

Standard cards are great. Foil and holographic editions make it truly special.

5

Order & Share

Cards arrive in 3–5 days. Share your card link for supporters to order their own copy.
