9. Blockchain Recording and Royalties

Each card's provenance (athlete name, card edition, serial number, and issuance date) is recorded on a public blockchain. This record is permanent and cannot be deleted. No personal contact information, financial information, or photographs are recorded on the blockchain.

Athletes are entitled to a 5% royalty on each verified secondary market resale of their card, facilitated through a smart contract. MVP Kids Cards makes reasonable efforts to enforce this royalty but cannot guarantee enforcement on all secondary platforms or informal sales. Royalty payments are disbursed to the wallet or account designated by the parent.
